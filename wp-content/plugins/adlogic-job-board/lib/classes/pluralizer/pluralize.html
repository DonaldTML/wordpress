Note: This is a pluralizer for English words only.
Examples (Plural --> Singular)
part-of-text ----> parts-of-text
child ----> children
dog's ----> dogs'
wolf ----> wolves
bear ----> bear
kitchen knife ----> kitchen knives
octopus (classical=Yes) ----> octopodes
octopus (classical=No) ----> octopuses
matrix (classical=Yes) ----> matrices
matrix (classical=No) ----> matrixes
my ( Noun) ----> our
my ( adjective) ----> my
